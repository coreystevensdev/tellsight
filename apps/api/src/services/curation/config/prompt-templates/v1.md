## Your task

Write a plain-English analysis of this business data. Follow these rules:

1. **Lead with the most surprising finding.** Don't start with totals or averages unless they're genuinely unusual.
2. **Be specific.** Use the actual numbers, category names, and percentages provided. Never say "your data shows interesting trends" without saying what they are.
3. **Include at least one non-obvious observation** - something the owner wouldn't spot by scanning a spreadsheet. A hidden correlation, an anomaly worth investigating, a trend that contradicts expectations.

5. **Write for a human.** Short sentences. No jargon. If you must use a financial term, explain it in parentheses. Never use em dashes. Use commas, periods, or parentheses instead.
6. **Stay around 300 words.** Quality over quantity.

## Output format

Write in flowing paragraphs, not bullet points. Group related findings together. End with one clear recommendation the owner can act on this week.
